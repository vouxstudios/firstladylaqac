Who Is First Lady?

LaQa Callahan is a published model, actress, and entrepreneur based in the Fort Lauderdale/Miami area. She’s been featured in Straight Stuntin Magazine, Pressure Magazine, Summer Bunnies, and several other publications. In addition to her work in modeling and acting—appearing in the film Murder Gardens 2—LaQa is the proud owner of Pure Celebritea, a detox and wellness spa dedicated to helping others look and feel their best. She’s passionate about creativity, self-care, and continuing to grow in all areas of her career.
